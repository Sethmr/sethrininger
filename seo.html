My Process

Every engagement starts with deep research. I don't use cookie-cutter SEO playbooks — I specialize in the client's industry, study their competitive landscape, and build a custom strategy that combines my engineering background with AI-powered analysis.

1

Industry Deep-Dive

I immerse myself in the client's industry — studying competitors, analyzing search patterns, and understanding what their customers actually search for. My years of experience across construction, tech, education, and small business give me a unique cross-industry perspective.

2

AI-Augmented Research

Using multiple AI platforms simultaneously (Claude, ChatGPT, Grok), I cross-reference insights, generate content strategies, and analyze competitive gaps that single-tool approaches miss entirely. This is my edge — AI as a force multiplier, not a replacement for expertise.

3

Technical Foundation

As a developer, I don't just audit technical SEO — I fix it. Schema markup, Core Web Vitals, site architecture, crawlability. Most SEO consultants file tickets. I push commits.

4

Google Business Profile Optimization

For local businesses, GBP is the front door. I optimize every field, build a review strategy, manage the Q&A, post regular updates, and ensure map pack visibility for the searches that matter most.

5

Content & Local Authority

Creating content that serves real people and satisfies search engines. Local landing pages, service area content, blog posts that answer the questions your customers are already asking.

6

Measure, Iterate, Report

Monthly reporting with clear metrics: ranking changes, traffic growth, conversion improvements. No vanity metrics — only data that reflects real business impact. Then we iterate on what's working.

⚡

The AI Advantage

Multiple AI platforms are part of my daily workflow — Claude, ChatGPT, and Grok each bring different strengths. I use them to analyze competitor pages, generate content briefs, identify keyword opportunities that traditional tools miss, and validate strategies across multiple perspectives before implementing. Combined with a decade of engineering discipline, this means faster iteration with higher precision.
