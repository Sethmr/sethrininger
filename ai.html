Case Study: AI-Powered Physical Problem Solving

How I used AI to fix a broken bed remote with 3D printing

My adjustable bed remote's buttons had degraded and stopped working. Rather than spending $50+ on a replacement remote, I used AI to design and 3D print replacement button covers — for pennies in filament.

Step 1: Claude AI

Described the problem and measurements. Claude generated OpenSCAD code for the replacement parts.

→

Step 2: OpenSCAD

Rendered and refined the 3D model, iterating on dimensions until the fit was precise.

→

Step 3: STL Export

Exported the verified model as an STL file for 3D printing.

→

Step 4: G-Code

Sliced the STL into printer instructions. Printed on a consumer 3D printer.

→

Step 5: Result

Perfect-fit replacement buttons. Remote works like new.
