Meet Brandi

Brandi is my wife — and honestly, the most driven person I know. She's a real estate broker based in the mountains of Western North Carolina, and she brings the same warmth, energy, and heart to her clients that she brings to our family every single day.

I built this page because I'm proud of her. Before real estate, Brandi spent a decade as a customer service manager at Publix — where she learned how to take care of people at a level most agents never reach. When she made the leap into real estate, that foundation showed immediately. She earned the Rookie of the Year award in WNC, and it didn't happen by accident — it came from late nights, early mornings, and a refusal to ever give a client anything less than her best.

Whether you're buying your first home, selling a family property, or searching for land in the Blue Ridge, Brandi brings a personal touch that's rare in this industry. She doesn't just close deals — she genuinely cares about getting it right for the people she works with.
